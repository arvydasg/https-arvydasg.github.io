Obelsdumas E-shop

Link - https://obelsdumas.lt/

E-shop for home-made Lithuanian meat products in Birzai. A client wanted a quick/cheap and easily maintainable solution, therefore Wordpress was chosen for this job.

Done by me:

  • Communication with the client
  • Site design
  • Building the site from scratch according to the needs
  • Setting up the payment solution
  • Hosting the site to the live environment
  • Maintaining the site on a monthly basis

My very first client.
